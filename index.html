Our Projects

Karim's Flyers and Ramadan Cards

We successfully printed 50,000 flyers for Karim’s marketing campaign in Bangalore, ensuring vibrant colors and premium quality to capture the attention of potential customers. In addition, we produced 5,000 Ramadan greeting cards, delivered on a tight schedule to support their festive outreach.

Cricket Crazy League - Banners, Certificates, and Caps

For the Cricket Crazy League Tournament, we provided complete event branding solutions. This included large format banners, participation certificates, custom name stickers for trophies, and caps printed with Direct-to-Fabric technology. Our high-quality prints and quick turnaround helped make the event a standout success.

MTR Menu Cards

We partnered with MTR for the launch of their new outlet in Dubai, delivering 15,000 high-quality menu cards. Designed and printed to reflect MTR's rich culinary heritage, premium dining experience and an elegant finish to enhance customer engagement.

DSR Group - Corporate Gift Boxes

We curated and produced 1,000 corporate gift boxes for DSR Group. Each box included custom-engraved diaries, thermal bottles, and premium pens, designed to leave a lasting impression. The entire package was tailored to reflect DSR Group’s brand identity, ensuring a perfect blend of functionality and style.
